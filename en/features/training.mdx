Supa's training module lets you convert existing SOPs, PDFs, or written procedures into structured mobile courses your team completes on their phone. This page explains how to build courses, add knowledge checks, track progress, and deploy training to multilingual frontline teams.

## Key capabilities

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="AI course builder">
    Paste text or upload a document and Supa AI structures it into a full course with modules, lesson content, and quiz questions. What used to take days of instructional design now takes minutes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Rich content types">
    Each lesson can include text, images, and video. Mix formats to keep learners engaged and match the complexity of the material.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Knowledge checks and quizzes">
    Add quizzes after each module to verify understanding before learners advance. Set a minimum passing score; Supa blocks progression until the worker meets it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Progress tracking">
    The training dashboard shows exactly who has started, completed, and passed each course — down to individual quiz scores. Managers can filter by location, team, or date range.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Completion certificates">
    Supa issues a completion certificate automatically when a worker finishes a course. Certificates are timestamped and stored in the worker's profile for compliance records.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Multi-language translation">
    Translate any course into 15 languages with one click. Each team member sees course content in their preferred language automatically, with no duplicate course management required.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Scheduled delivery and auto-enrollment">
    Assign courses to new hires automatically on day one. Set any course to enroll workers based on their role, location, or hire date — no manual assignment needed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="In-workflow micro-lessons">
    Embed short lessons directly inside daily checklist workflows. Workers complete a micro-lesson as part of their routine without switching to a separate training section.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Creating a course

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Training module">
    Log in to the Supa web dashboard and go to **Training** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a new course">
    Click **New Course** and give it a name and description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate content with AI or build manually">
    Click **Generate with AI** and paste your SOP text or upload a PDF. Supa AI produces structured modules and lesson content. Alternatively, build each module manually using the course editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add quizzes and knowledge checks">
    At the end of each module, add a quiz. Set question types (multiple choice, true/false) and define the minimum passing score.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign to team members or set auto-enrollment">
    Select specific workers, entire locations, or configure auto-enrollment rules based on role or start date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track completion">
    Monitor who has started, completed, and passed each course from the **Training** dashboard. Send reminders to incomplete learners with one click.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Translating a course

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the course">
    Navigate to the course you want to translate in the **Training** module.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Translate">
    Select **Translate** from the course settings menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select target languages">
    Choose from 15 supported languages including Vietnamese, English, Spanish, Chinese, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Supa AI translates all content. Workers automatically see the course in their preferred language with no additional steps.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  AI-generated course content is always editable. Review each module before publishing to confirm accuracy, especially for safety-critical or compliance-sensitive material.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Supa delivers 10,000\+ lessons daily across the platform. Use the completion dashboard to identify courses with low pass rates and refine the content.
</Tip>
