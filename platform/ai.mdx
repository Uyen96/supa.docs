Supa AI is a built-in assistant that eliminates the two biggest time costs in operations management: creating content and finding information. Instead of spending hours writing checklists and training courses from scratch, you describe what you need and Supa AI builds it. Instead of frontline staff flipping through a binder or searching a shared drive, they ask a question and get a direct answer from your uploaded SOPs.

## Generate a checklist with AI

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the checklist builder">
    Go to **Templates > New Checklist** and click **Generate with AI**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe your process">
    Type a description of what the checklist should cover. Be specific about the context — the more detail you provide, the more relevant the output. For example: "Daily opening checklist for a milk tea cafe — covers equipment startup, ingredient prep, cleanliness, and cash register."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the generated checklist">
    Supa AI produces a full checklist with item types (photo capture, numeric entry, text confirmation, dropdown) already assigned based on the nature of each step. Review each item, edit wording, reorder steps, or delete anything that does not apply.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure and publish">
    Set the geofence, assign to locations, and publish. The entire process from description to published checklist typically takes under five minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Generate a training course from a document

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the course builder">
    Go to **Training > Courses > New Course** and click **Generate with AI**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your document">
    Upload a PDF, Word file (.docx), or plain text file. Supa AI accepts manuals, SOPs, training guides, policy documents, and similar materials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the generated course">
    Supa AI breaks the document into logical modules, writes a brief guide for each, and generates quiz questions to test understanding. Review the structure, adjust module titles, edit quiz questions, and add or remove content as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign and publish">
    Assign the course to the relevant roles or locations and publish. Staff receive a notification that the course is ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Upload your existing SOP PDFs — even older ones that have never been formatted for training — to get AI-generated courses in minutes. You can always refine the output, but AI course generation gives you a complete starting point instantly rather than a blank page.
</Tip>

## Document Q&A for frontline staff

Supa AI can answer questions from your frontline team based on documents you have uploaded to the SOP library. Staff access this from the Supa mobile app by tapping **Ask Supa AI** and typing their question.

Example questions staff can ask:
- "What is the correct temperature for storing dairy products?"
- "How do I handle a customer complaint about a food quality issue?"
- "What are the steps for opening the store if the manager is not on site?"

Supa AI reads your uploaded documents and returns a direct, sourced answer — citing which document and section the answer came from. If the answer is not in any uploaded document, Supa AI says so rather than guessing.

To add a document to the Q&A library, go to **Training > SOP Library > Upload Document** and toggle **Enable for AI Q&A**.

## Supported languages

Supa AI translation supports 15+ languages including:

English, Vietnamese, Chinese (Simplified), Chinese (Traditional), Japanese, Korean, Thai, Bahasa Indonesia, Bahasa Malaysia, Tagalog, Hindi, Arabic, French, Spanish, and Portuguese.

To translate a checklist or course, open it and click **Translate > Select language**. Supa AI generates the translated version as a separate copy that you can review and publish independently.
