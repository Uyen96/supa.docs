Most training systems sit outside the work itself — a separate app, a classroom session, or a PDF nobody reads. Supa embeds training directly into the checklists and tasks your team already uses every day. Staff learn at the moment they need the knowledge, confirm understanding with a quiz, then complete the task. There is no context switching and no separate training session to schedule.

## Create a training module

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the course builder">
    In Supa, go to **Training > Courses** and click **New Course**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a title and description">
    Give the course a clear name (e.g., "Food Hygiene — Level 1") and a short description. Choose the role or location this course is for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your modules">
    Click **Add module**. Each module can contain:
    - A video (upload an MP4 or paste a link)
    - A written guide with images
    - A step-by-step walkthrough
    - A quiz with multiple-choice or true/false questions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the completion order">
    Toggle **Sequential completion** if staff must finish each module before unlocking the next. Leave it off to let staff complete modules in any order.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign to roles or locations">
    Under **Assign**, choose which roles or locations should complete this course. You can also set a completion deadline.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Click **Publish**. Supa notifies assigned staff and the course appears in their **My Training** list on mobile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Attach training to a checklist step

The micro-learning in workflow feature lets you link a training module — or a single piece of content — to any individual checklist item. Here is how:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the checklist template">
    Go to **Templates**, open the checklist you want to enhance, and click on the item you want to attach training to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a learning attachment">
    Click **Attach training**, then choose an existing module from your SOP library or upload new content (video, image, or document).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set as required or optional">
    Mark the attachment as **Required** if staff must view it before they can complete the item. Set it to **Optional** if it is reference material only.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and publish">
    Save the item. The next time staff encounter this checklist step, they will see the training content before the completion input.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Attach a 60–90 second video and a two-question quiz to any checklist item that involves a safety-critical or brand-standard task. Staff watch the video, pass the quiz, and then complete the task — confirming understanding before they act.
</Tip>

## AI-powered course generation

If you have existing documentation — a PDF hygiene manual, a Word-format SOP, or a slide deck — Supa AI can convert it into a structured training course automatically.

In **Training > Courses**, click **Generate with AI**, upload your document, and Supa AI will:
- Break the content into logical modules
- Write quiz questions for each module
- Format everything as a ready-to-publish course

Review and edit the generated content before publishing. This typically takes minutes rather than the hours required to build a course manually.

## Tracking completion and certification

The **Training dashboard** shows a real-time view of team progress:

- **Completion rate** — percentage of assigned staff who have finished each course
- **Quiz scores** — individual and average scores per module
- **Certification status** — whether each staff member holds a current certification for a given course
- **Overdue** — staff who have not completed assigned training by the deadline

Filter by location, role, course, or individual. Export the report as a CSV or PDF for compliance audits or HR reviews.
