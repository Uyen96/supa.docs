Running a retail chain means trusting that dozens — or hundreds — of stores are executing your standards every single day. When that trust breaks down, you find out too late: a planogram deviation that lived for two weeks, a cash reconciliation that was never done, a store opening that was ticked off from the car park. Supa closes that gap with GPS-locked checklists, live photo capture, and a single dashboard that shows you exactly what's happening at every location right now.

## The challenge

Retail chains lose up to **30% of operational productivity** from inconsistent SOP compliance. The root causes are predictable: area managers spend hours each week driving to stores to verify work that should already be verified, frontline staff learn quickly that unchecked checklists go unnoticed, and paper-based or screenshot-based "proof" is trivially easy to fake.

By the time a visual merchandising deviation surfaces in a quarterly audit, it's been impacting sales for weeks. By the time a cash discrepancy is caught, it's compounded. The audit finds symptoms; you need a system that prevents the problem.

## How Supa solves it

Supa gives retail chains a single platform to assign, verify, and improve daily operations across every store. Three capabilities make the difference:

- **GPS-locked checklists** — a checklist can only be submitted from within a configured radius of the store. Managers at head office see a live map of completions; no submission from the car park counts.
- **Live photo capture** — checklist steps can require a timestamped photo taken in-app. Staff cannot upload an old image from their camera roll; every photo carries a timestamp and GPS coordinate.
- **Real-time area manager visibility** — instead of driving to stores, area managers review photo evidence and compliance scores from a single dashboard, then escalate exceptions as tasks assigned directly to the responsible person.

<Note>
Supa's AI can generate a ready-to-use store opening or planogram audit checklist from your existing SOP document in seconds. Paste your SOP or upload a PDF and Supa produces a structured checklist you can deploy immediately.
</Note>
