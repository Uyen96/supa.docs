Managing operations across multiple locations means trusting that frontline staff are following your standards — but trust isn't verification. Supa closes the gap between what gets reported and what actually happens on the ground, giving operations teams real-time proof that work was done, at the right place, at the right time.

## What Supa is

Supa is an all-in-one operations platform built for multi-location businesses in retail, F&B, hospitality, and facilities management. From a single dashboard, your head office can create standardized checklists and SOPs, push them to dozens or thousands of locations instantly, and receive live, GPS-verified photo proof that every task was completed — no paperwork, no guesswork, no pencil-whipping.

The platform combines five operational layers into one mobile-first tool:

- **Verified execution** — GPS geofencing and live-camera-only capture confirm staff are on-site and tasks are genuinely done
- **Task and workflow management** — assign ad-hoc or recurring tasks to specific roles, locations, or individuals with automatic escalation when deadlines are missed
- **Training and SOPs** — deliver micro-courses and SOP-linked learning inside daily workflows so staff learn while they work
- **AI-powered operations** — generate checklists, translate content into 15+ languages, and get instant answers from your operational manuals
- **Analytics and insights** — track compliance scores by store, spot systemic bottlenecks, and export reports for leadership review

## The problem Supa solves

In any distributed operation, there is always a gap between what managers assume is happening and what is actually happening on the floor. Paper checklists get backdated. Photo "proof" gets pulled from camera rolls. Staff check off tasks they haven't done. The result is a false picture of compliance that only surfaces when a customer complaint, a failed audit, or a health-and-safety incident makes the gap impossible to ignore.

Supa eliminates this gap by making unverified reporting technically impossible. GPS geofencing means a check cannot be submitted unless the staff member is physically inside the location perimeter. Live-camera-only capture means photos must be taken in the moment — gallery uploads are blocked at the app level. Every submission carries an automatic timestamp and GPS coordinate, creating a tamper-proof audit trail.

## Who uses Supa

Supa is designed for every level of a multi-location organization:

- **Operations Directors and COOs** — need full-network compliance visibility and systemic bottleneck data to improve execution at scale
- **Area Managers** — need real-time completion dashboards and automated alerts when critical tasks are missed at locations in their patch
- **Frontline staff** — need a simple, guided mobile interface that tells them exactly what to do, in what order, with embedded training when needed

Supa works across chains of 10 locations and networks of 1,000+. It is available on iOS and Android, and is designed to run smoothly on any smartphone.

## By the numbers

Supa customers collectively complete over **1 million checks per year** and deliver more than **10,000 micro-learning lessons every day**. Organizations using Supa report up to **40% savings** from improvements in safety and compliance outcomes.
