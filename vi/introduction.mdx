Supa is an AI-powered operations platform built for distributed frontline teams. This page explains what Supa is, the problems it solves, and how it fits your organization — whether you manage five locations or five hundred.

## The problem with distributed operations

When your team is spread across multiple sites, operational blind spots are inevitable. Managers rely on self-reported task completion, paper checklists get lost or faked, and by the time a compliance issue surfaces, the damage is already done. Traditional tools weren't designed for workers who don't sit at a desk.

Supa closes that gap by giving you verified, timestamped, GPS-confirmed proof that work was done — from the field, in real time.

## What Supa does

Supa connects your management team to your frontline workers through two interfaces: a **web dashboard** for managers and admins, and a **mobile app** for the workers on the ground. Everything that happens in the field flows back to the dashboard automatically.
